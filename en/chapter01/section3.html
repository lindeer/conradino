Ludwig von Baiern

Given that Konradin was immature for many years, it was of immeasurable importance for him to find a lawyer who would take care of his rights with care and energy. Such was Duke Ludwig, his guardian in Germany. Next to him, the younger and weaker Duke Heinrich took a back seat. Ludwig II the Strict is one of the most powerful rulers of his time. The dreadful deed to which the irascibility led him to arouse bitter repentance, but it did not break his strong nature. Equipped with intelligent statesmanlike talent and military ability, he restlessly and willingly endeavored at all times to satisfy his own ambition and pride of dynasty at the same time by expanding his country and promoting his subjects). He probably looked at his connection with the Hohenstaufen from the same point of view. Certainly the representation of the interests of Konradin created enough worries and troubles for him, but just as undoubtedly it strengthened his own power. Not only did he gain decisive influence over the Hohenstaufen supporters, but he was also able to put the weight of Hohenstaufen rights and claims into the balance in his relationships with other rulers, wherever he considered it appropriate. 1) Selflessness and loving care were hardly the mainsprings of his actions against his nephew; in this respect he will have made no prominent exception from the princes of his time. But precisely because the support of Konradin was for him at the same time a commandment of selfishness, it became all the more energetic and reliable.2)
